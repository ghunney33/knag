Our Story

KNAG Industries International is the parent company for a diverse portfolio of innovative businesses spanning various industry sectors. Collectively, the Group and its affiliated companies boast over two decades of cross-sector expertise in strategic engagement and global opportunity identification, consistently driving business success. We currently have interests in or investments in the following domains/industries:

  • Logistics
  • Technology
  • Philanthropy
  • Agro-processing
  • Construction
  • Energy and Power
  • Petroleum Products

We thrive on our commitment and dedication to Accountability, Service, Reliability and Value Creation. Join us.
